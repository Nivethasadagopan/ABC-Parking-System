What is a Flexi Premier Card?

Flexi Card

Flexi Premier Cards are perfect for part-time commuters or visitors who don’t need parking every day, usable at conveniently located city centre car parks across Ireland. As part of your Flexi Premier Cards you'll receive: A set number of hours (90 or 120) to use across multiple visits. Full flexibility to park when it suits your schedule. Convenient online account management. Entry and exit with your number plate, no need for paper tickets! Your parking just got even easier.
